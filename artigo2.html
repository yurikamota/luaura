Rulerships and the Nature of the Planets in Jyotish


Understand where planets rule and how they behave in a Vedic birth chart


What are rulerships?

In Vedic astrology, each planet rules one or two zodiac signs. This means the planet holds power and authority over that sign — like a king in its own kingdom. It naturally expresses itself well there and resonates with the sign’s energy.

Planetary Rulerships

PlanetSign(s) Ruled
SunLeo (Simha)
MoonCancer (Karka)
MarsAries (Mesha), Scorpio (Vrischika)
MercuryGemini (Mithuna), Virgo (Kanya)
JupiterSagittarius (Dhanu), Pisces (Meena)
VenusTaurus (Vrishabha), Libra (Tula)
SaturnCapricorn (Makara), Aquarius (Kumbha)
RahuNone (exalted in Taurus or Gemini, depending on tradition)
KetuNone (exalted in Scorpio or Sagittarius)

Note: Rahu and Ketu don’t rule signs because they are shadow planets (lunar nodes), but they still exert a powerful influence in the birth chart.

The Nature of the Planets

Each planet also has a natural disposition (prakriti), which affects how it behaves in a chart:

Natural Benefics (Shubha Grahas)


  • Jupiter – Wisdom, spirituality, expansion, protection.
  • Venus – Love, beauty, pleasure, harmony.
  • Mercury – Intellect, communication (can be neutral depending on company).
  • Moon (full) – Emotions, nurturing, motherhood.

Natural Malefics (Papa Grahas)


  • Saturn – Limits, discipline, delays, purposeful struggle.
  • Mars – Action, aggression, courage (can also bring conflict).
  • Sun – Ego, authority, can “burn” what it touches.
  • Rahu and Ketu – Illusion, karmic patterns, obsessions and liberation.
    (They are naturally malefic, but carry deep spiritual lessons.)

Summary

Rulership shows where a planet holds influence.
Nature shows how the planet acts — benefic, malefic, or neutral.
The house, sign, and aspects will further shape its energy — a planet may behave very differently depending on its context in the chart.
